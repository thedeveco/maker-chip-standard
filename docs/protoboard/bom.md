## Pad Components (PCB Features, No Supplier)

These are exposed copper pads on the bottom PCB — fabricated as part of the board, not purchased separately:

- Pad 1 — VCC (5 V), 3 mm
- Pad 2 — GND, 3 mm
- Pad 3 — DATA, 3 mm
- Pad 4 — CLK, 3 mm
- Pad 5 — CS/RST, 3 mm
- Pad 6 — MISO/GPIO, 3 mm
- Pad 7 — GPIO/PWM, 3 mm
- Pad 8 — ANALOG, 3 mm
